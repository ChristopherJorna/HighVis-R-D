**Used for:** Cityscape scene — depth map video → AI video gen
**Type:** Video generation prompt
**Input:** Magnific upscaled image + depth map video (CityscapeDepth.mp4)
**Constraint discovered:** Video gen systems capped at 10 seconds max

```
Use the input video as the only source of motion and composition. Apply the identical camera movement from the input
video with zero deviation. Match camera position, camera rotation, camera speed, easing, and timing exactly. The
scene must behave as if the same camera rig was reused on the same tripod.

All terrain silhouettes, building outlines, horizon line, and depth layers visible in the first frame must remain in
the exact same position, scale, and spatial relationship throughout the entire shot. No shape changes, no layout
shifts, no scale changes, and no procedural motion on any element.

Background and environment constraints: The composition, horizon placement, terrain silhouette, and building outline
positions must remain identical to the input video in every frame. Do not replace, enhance, reinterpret, or alter the
layout. No new buildings, terrain features, or environmental elements may be introduced. Preserve original framing,
perspective, and spatial depth at all times.

The only transformation allowed is replacing the visual appearance with a hyperrealistic Riyadh skyline at night
viewed from moonlit desert. Sky: dark brown-orange from city light pollution, warm atmospheric haze above the city.
City: Kingdom Centre tower with blue-white illuminated crown, Al Faisaliah tower with green accent lighting, dense
skyscrapers glowing amber and gold, orange sodium streetlight bloom across all roads, volumetric city glow, real glass
and concrete textures. Foreground: cool moonlit desert sand dunes, fine-grained ochre sand, directional blue-silver
moonlight shadows, no artificial foreground light. Light bloom and lens halation on all bright sources. Temporally
stable — no flickering, no texture drift between frames, no grain, no noise.

Hyperrealistic, cinematic, 8K. Anamorphic lens. Not CGI, not illustrated, not a video game. Hard constraint: camera
transform is immutable throughout the shot.
```
